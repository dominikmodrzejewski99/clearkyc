ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION

of

1. Registered Name and Status

1.1 The name of the Company is Bosphorus Trading FZE (the "Company"), a Free Zone Establishment incorporated under the rules of the Al-Marisa Free Zone Authority, Emirate of Qaram.
1.2 The Company is a single-shareholder company limited by shares.

2.1 The registered office of the Company shall be situated at Office 1408, Al-Marisa Free Zone Business Centre, Emirate of Qaram, being the offices of the Company's licensed free zone agent, Qaram Corporate Services LLC.
2.2 The Company does not maintain any physical warehouse or staffed retail premises and holds a flexi-desk licence only.

3. Business Activity

3.1 The stated business activity of the Company is "general trading and commercial brokerage of consumer goods."
3.2 The Company has no employees on record. Company secretarial and administrative functions are performed exclusively by the licensed free zone agent referenced in Clause 2.1.

4. Directors

4.1 The board of directors of the Company as of the date of these Articles comprises the following person:
Name: Ilker A. Doganay   |   Role: Sole Director   |   Appointed: 2 September 2021

5. Share Capital and Beneficial Ownership

5.1 The issued share capital of the Company is 150 ordinary shares of USD 100.00 each, fully paid.
5.2 The entire issued share capital of the Company is held directly by Ilker A. Doganay, who holds 100% of the issued shares and is recorded as the sole ultimate beneficial owner in the free zone licence file.
5.3 Mr. Doganay's licence file discloses his status as a politically exposed person by virtue of his public office referenced in Clause 4.2. The source of the initial share capital is stated in the file as "personal savings," with no supporting documentation of the origin of funds on record.

7. Registered Agent and Correspondence

7.1 All statutory correspondence for the Company shall be directed to Qaram Corporate Services LLC at the address stated in Clause 2.1. The Company confirms no alternative correspondence address is maintained.

8. Powers of the Company

8.1 Subject to the rules of the Al-Marisa Free Zone Authority, the Company has full capacity to carry on or undertake any licensed trading activity and, for these purposes, full rights, powers, and privileges.

9.1 The financial year of the Company ends on 31 December. The Company is not required under the Al-Marisa Free Zone Authority's rules to file audited financial statements and none have been produced with this filing.
